1. Purpose and Scope

Alberta's Health Compass is a healthcare intelligence platform designed to provide authorized users with access to provincial health data for decision-making, analysis, and operational improvement. By accessing this system, you agree to use it responsibly and in accordance with all applicable laws and regulations.

2. Authorized Use

You confirm that you are an authorized user with legitimate business need to access this system. Authorized users include:

  • Healthcare administrators and executives
  • Clinical directors and managers
  • Health system analysts and researchers
  • Government officials with health portfolio responsibilities

3. Data Privacy and Confidentiality

All data accessed through this platform is confidential and subject to privacy legislation including the Health Information Act (HIA) and Freedom of Information and Protection of Privacy Act (FOIP). You agree to:

  • Maintain strict confidentiality of all accessed information
  • Not share login credentials with unauthorized individuals
  • Not extract, copy, or distribute data without proper authorization
  • Report any suspected privacy breaches immediately

4. Acceptable Use

You agree to use this system only for legitimate healthcare planning, analysis, and decision-making purposes. Prohibited activities include:

  • Attempting to identify individual patients
  • Using data for commercial purposes without authorization
  • Accessing data outside your scope of responsibility
  • Attempting to circumvent security measures

5. System Monitoring

All system usage is logged and monitored for security, audit, and quality assurance purposes. Your queries, accessed data, and system interactions may be reviewed to ensure compliance with these terms.

6. Limitation of Liability

While we strive for accuracy, you acknowledge that data may contain errors or limitations. Clinical and operational decisions should incorporate appropriate validation and professional judgment. Alberta Health Services is not liable for decisions made based solely on information from this system.
